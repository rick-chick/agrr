Report structure â€‹

Each crop report is organized into the following sections:

1. Environmental requirements â€‹

Temperature requirements â€‹

For each growth stage (germination, vegetative growth, flowering, fruiting), we summarize:

  • optimal_min / optimal_max: optimal minimum and maximum temperatures
  • base_temperature: base temperature (growth start threshold)
  • low_stress_threshold: low-temperature stress threshold
  • high_stress_threshold: high-temperature stress threshold
  • max_temperature: maximum temperature
  • frost_threshold: frost damage threshold
  • sterility_risk_threshold: sterility risk threshold (flowering only)

GDD requirements â€‹

Guidelines for growth stage progression based on Growing Degree Days (GDD).

2. Nutrition requirements â€‹

NPK uptake profiles for each growth stage.

3. Pests & diseases â€‹

Major pests and practical countermeasures.
